Dashing Cards

This is a card

Dashing Cards are used to contain and display any type of content.

This is an example of a simple card. It is convenient because it requires very little markup to use.

This is a card with an <hr>

Use an <hr> to provide greater visual separation between your card header and content. Use the class .card--hr-full to span your <hr> edge to edge.

Using an <hr> without the .card--hr-full class name will allow you to separate content within your card, while still respecting your $grid-gutter.

This is a selectable card

This is a card with a form

Cards are also great for containing form elements. When placing a form within a card, be sure to give the card a class of .card--no-padding and give the columns a class of .column--nested. This will allow you to retain a consistent grid-gutter when including .row and .column elements.
